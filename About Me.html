About Me

Hello, and welcome to my corner of the web!

I am Lewis Okoth, a passionate and diligent software engineer currently undertaking my studies at Multimedia University. Being in software development for over two years, I have had an exposition to a wide set of projects that has fostered both my technical skills and knowledge of leading-edge technologies.

I am driven in the tech world by a love for problem-solving with efficient and user-friendly solutions. Whether that be designing responsive websites, developing intuitive mobile applications, or automating complex processes, I thrive on challenges that push me creatively and drive me to innovate.

Here's a snapshot of what I do:

  • Web Development: I create dynamic, interactive web applications using modern frameworks like React and JavaScript to ensure smooth user experiences.
  • Mobile App Development: I do cross-platform app development using React Native, ensuring smooth functionality for iOS and Android devices.
  • Automation and Scripting: Saving time by streamlining workflow is my strong suit. I create scripts and automation tools that enhance efficiency.
  • Consultation and Technical Support: I’m passionate about helping others navigate the tech world, whether it’s through mentorship, troubleshooting, or offering practical advice.

Beyond just the technical, I'm a firm believer in the magic of sharing. The blog is where I keep notes on my journey and share insights and simplify some concepts around software engineering. From setting up simple-step-by-step tutorials, coding challenges, and giving tips and tricks to make tech friendly for both beginners and pros.

In a field that’s constantly evolving, I’m committed to lifelong learning. My toolbox includes technologies like React, Node.js, Python, MongoDB, and more, and I’m always exploring new tools to stay ahead of the curve.

When I’m not coding, you’ll find me brainstorming ideas, collaborating on exciting projects, or diving into the latest tech trends. I’m always open to connecting with like-minded individuals, potential collaborators, or anyone passionate about technology.

Let's connect, learn, and create something amazing together.
